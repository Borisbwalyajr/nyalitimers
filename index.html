How To Use Our Electronic Scales

Below are the three simple steps on how to use our electronic scales

Power On and Calibrate
Turn on the scale and ensure it is placed on a flat, stable surface. Allow it to self-calibrate or manually set it to zero for accurate readings.
Place the Item
Gently place the item to be weighed in the center of the scale’s platform. Ensure nothing is touching the item or scale during the process.
Read and Record
Wait for the scale to stabilize, then read the displayed weight. Record the measurement or proceed with your next item.
